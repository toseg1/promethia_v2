Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 1, 2025
Important: This privacy policy explains how Promethia collects, uses, and protects your personal data in compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and other applicable data protection laws.

1. Data Controller

Promethia
Email: theo.seguin@promethia.app

2. What Data We Collect

2.1 Account Information

  • Basic Account Data: Username, email address, password (encrypted);
  • Role Information: Whether you're registered as a coach or athlete;
  • Profile Information: Full name, date of birth, phone number, bio, profile picture (optional);
  • Sports Information: Certifications and/or Achievements (optional).

2.2 Training Data

  • Performance Metrics: MAS (Maximal Aerobic Speed), FTP (Functional Threshold Power), CSS (Critical Swim Speed) (optional);
  • Training Sessions: Workout details, duration, intensity, notes, and related data;
  • Calendar Events: Training schedules, race dates, and custom events.

3. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal data based on the following:

  • Contract Performance: To provide our training management services;
  • Legitimate Interest: To improve our services and ensure security;
  • Consent: For coach-athlete data sharing relationships (see Section 5);
  • Legal Obligation: To comply with applicable laws and regulations.

4. How We Use Your Data

4.1 Core Services

  • Provide training calendar and session management;
  • Store and display your training data and race results;
  • Calculate performance metrics and progress tracking;
  • Enable communication between coaches and athletes.

4.2 Service Improvement

  • Analyze usage patterns to improve user experience;
  • Develop new features based on user needs;
  • Ensure platform security and prevent abuse.
Important: Promethia does NOT commercialize your data. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

5. Coach-Athlete Data Sharing

5.1 Consent for Data Sharing

When you accept a coach relationship, you explicitly consent to allow your assigned coach to:

  • View your training sessions, performance data and profile;
  • Access your calendar and schedule training sessions;
  • View your performance metrics (MAS, FTP, CSS) and progress;
  • Add training sessions and events to your calendar;
  • View your profile information relevant to training.

5.2 Coach Data Access Limitations

Coaches can only access data from athletes who have explicitly accepted the coach relationship. Coaches cannot:

  • Access data from athletes who haven't accepted the relationship;
  • Share your data with other parties without your consent;
  • Use your data for commercial purposes.

5.3 Withdrawing Consent

You can withdraw your consent and remove coach access at any time by:

  • Ending the coach-athlete relationship in your account settings;
  • Contacting us at theo.seguin@promethia.app

6. Data Retention

  • Active Accounts: We retain your data while your account is active;
  • Deleted Accounts: Data is permanently deleted within 30 days of account deletion;
  • Training Data: Retained for the duration of your account to maintain training history;
  • Legal Requirements: Some data may be retained longer if required by law.

7. Your Rights Under GDPR

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

7.1 Right of Access

You can request a copy of all personal data we hold about you.

7.2 Right to Rectification

You can update or correct your personal data through your account settings or by contacting us.

7.3 Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten")

You can request deletion of your personal data. This can be done by:

  • Deleting your account through the app settings;
  • Contacting us at promethia.app@gmail.com

7.4 Right to Data Portability

You can request your data in a structured, machine-readable format.

7.5 Right to Restrict Processing

You can request that we limit how we process your data in certain circumstances.

7.6 Right to Object

You can object to processing based on legitimate interests.

To exercise your rights, contact us at:
Email: theo.seguin@promethia.app
Subject: "GDPR Data Request - [Your Request Type]"

8. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/SSL);
  • Secure password hashing;
  • Regular security updates and monitoring;
  • Limited access to personal data on a need-to-know basis.

9. Data Transfers

Your data is primarily stored within the European Economic Area (EEA) on Render servees. Profile Picture can be stored outside of the EEA on Cloudinary servers (the profile picture upload is optional).

10. Cookies and Tracking

We use essential cookies to:

  • Maintain your login session;
  • Remember your preferences;
  • Ensure site security.

We do not use tracking cookies for advertising or analytics without your consent.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by:

  • Email notification to your registered email address;
  • Prominent notice in the application;
  • Updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy.
